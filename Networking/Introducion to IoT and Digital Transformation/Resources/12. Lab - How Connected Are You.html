Lab - How Connected Are You?

Objectives

Survey 3 or 4 people: family, colleagues, and friends. Determine how many hours they are connected via any device during each day.

Background / Scenario

Computers, the internet, mobile phones, texting, and social networking have become routine to most of us. People who were born in the 90s are considered to be ‘digital natives’, which means that they have never known a time when these technologies did not exist. This survey will help you to recognize how much of the day is actually spent “connected”. This will help us to see the impact this generation will have on business and the economy.
